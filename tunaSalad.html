Home Tuna Salad

Ingredients

  • Tuna
  • Mayo
  • Picle relish
  • Mustard
  • Onion
  • Celery

Directions

    Place the tuna in a mixing bowl
  1. Put mayo, mustard, and pickle relish into the bowl
  2. Cut up some onion
  3. Put the diced onion into the bowl
  4. Cut up celery into small slices
  5. Put the celery into the bowl
  6. Mix it all together
