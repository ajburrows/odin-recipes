Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cut cheese into rectangular slices
  2. Butter one side of the bread for both slices
  3. Place one slice of bread onto the pan at medium heat
  4. Lay the cheese on the bread
  5. Place the other slice of bread on top
  6. Pour a little splash of water into the pan
  7. Cover the pan to trap the heat
  8. When the bread is finished toasting, flip the sandwich and cover again
